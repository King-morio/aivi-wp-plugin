What Is the Best Resume Format in 2023?

When writing a successful resume, format drives readability and trust. Employers need structured facts quickly, so layout choices directly affect how your value is understood.

What is the best format in 2023? With multiple styles available, the right choice depends on your work history, strengths, and how you want hiring teams to scan your profile.

The three main formats are chronological, functional, and combination. Chronological resumes present work in reverse order, functional resumes emphasize skills, and combination resumes balance both for broad review.
