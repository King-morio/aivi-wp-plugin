What AiVI actually does

What the plugin does, in plain English.

AiVI is a WordPress plugin for AI visibility. It analyzes your content before publication and shows where it may fail machine extraction, trust, structure, schema, and answer readiness.

In simple terms, AiVI helps content become more usable by AI systems, not just readable by people.

Why good content still gets missed

Strong writing alone does not guarantee strong performance in AI-assisted discovery.

A different problem from traditional SEO

A page can be factually correct, well-formatted, and still underperform in AI-driven discovery.

That usually happens because the content does not present answers clearly enough for extraction, does not support claims strongly enough for trust, or does not signal structure and schema cleanly enough for machines to interpret with confidence.

AiVI helps you see those gaps before the page goes live.

What AiVI helps you improve

  • Opening answers that are easier for AI systems to quote and reuse
  • Content structure that is easier to scan, interpret, and extract
  • Schema and semantic signals that better match what the page is trying to do
  • Trust signals that reduce weak, vague, or unsupported claims
  • Editorial decisions inside WordPress, before publication
